    The Big Academy

    The Big Academy is a recruitment platform which allows users to register, post vacancies, search vacancies and apply for them. Candidates can create profiles which act as a digital CV to promote themselves to employers in a modern and innovative way. The site also offers candidates career advice as well as a range of tools to help them in their working life such as a budget calculator, calendar and diary. I was responsible for coding up both the front and back end, as well as designing the database and managing a VPS used to host the site. At its core the site is powered by the CodeIgniter PHP framework with a MySQL database. This has been one of my largest projects to date and I'm currently still working on further developing the site.
